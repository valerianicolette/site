My drive to work at the intersection of policy and communications stems from believing that helping people understand the vital work of our government is essential for democracy - especially now. Government

Master's Thesis

Academic

Developed strong research, writing, and data visualization skills through graduate thesis work, focusing on making complex policy topics accessible to broader audiences.

It's one thing to complete a thesis, but it's another to practice presenting it and explaining complex topics in a way that's accessible for different audiences. As one of 10 McCourt Showcase finalists, I was able to do just that—creating a visually appealing poster and presentation that distilled my research into clear, engaging content that could resonate with both academic and general audiences.

The Approach

With over a decade in customer service, I’ve always valued the sense of community that great teams create — and Dacha Beer Garden is a perfect example. If you’ve been, you know how fun it is to be there; working there is just as joyful.

Alongside my role, I contributed to Dacha’s social media by creating content that captured its authentic energy — from highlighting seasonal food and drink specials to making reels that celebrated the people and moments that make the space special.

Skills: Community storytelling, brand personality development, local engagement

PERSONAL

My imagination extends far beyond professional work - I've always considered myself a creative person, and I genuinely love making things just because they bring me joy. Whether it's capturing moments from my travels, experimenting with video editing, making jewelry, playing ukulele, or even coding this website, these projects are pure expressions of who I am outside of work. My creative spirit is what drives everything I do.

Personal Videos

Through short films and video snippets, I document the energy and essence of everyday moments—turning fleeting experiences into cinematic memories that capture not just what happened, but how it felt.
