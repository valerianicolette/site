FINDING MYSELF IN COLOR

I didn't just pick colors I liked when desigining this website - I chose colors that reflect what I love most about the world around me. As an earth sign, I've always been drawn to groundedness, warmth, and organic beauty. I want my personality to shine through colors that capture the beauty and vibrance of our gardens, forests, oceans, and skies.

HOME

This deep magenta feels like coming home to myself. It's the color of flowers blooming at dusk, holding all the richness and warmth I want to create in every space I live in.

ABOUT

Bright raspberry is pure joy to me. It's that feeling when you're watching an incredible sunset and can't help but smile. This is how I try to show up: open-hearted, honest, and never afraid to be vulnerable if it means we can really connect.

MY STORY

This soft rose reminds me that strength doesn't have to be loud. Like delicate petals pushing through concrete, it's gentle but determined. When I share my story, I want it to feel tender but powerful, because that's where real trust begins.

WORLD

Fresh grass after rain! This bright green is that moment when everything feels possible again, when you remember we're all connected in ways we don't even realize. It holds all my hope for what we can build together.

EXPERIENCE

Forest green grounds me completely. Like having your hands in rich soil or walking through ancient trees. This color represents my commitment to climate work and the deep responsibility I feel to protect what sustains us all.

PORTFOLIO

Electric blue is pure possibility. It's that feeling when you look up at an endless sky and you suddenly remember that that's your limit. It's the color of imagination running wild and creating something that's never existed before.

CONTACT

Deep blue feels like the most honest conversation you've ever had. Calm, clear, genuine. This is the energy I want to bring when connecting with new people: steady, trustworthy, and genuinely excited to see what we might create together.
